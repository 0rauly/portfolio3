Wat is Kurkuma


Kurkuma is a long-term project who started as a simple idea of creating more poc characters in the animation industry. Kurkuma is a girl person with a different skin colour who is experimenting racism en colorism inside her own comunity.

With this experiment we, a group of young woc, are trying to have a small impact in this society. We want to give queer poc characters they can relate to and feel close to because we all grew up without an animation figure who resembled us. We never saw someone like US.

Throughout her journee Kurkuma meets new people...
People from different countries, etnicities, even people with dissabilities. 

Because we are a extremely small group we do not work fast.
But because we are not alone, we will go far.

The hardest past is the creation of the story. Characters and decor just goes with the flow and most of the time the entire group agrees on the desing of the characters. The story whoever changes everytime the group comes togheter. Chapter one took nearly 2 months because everyone had a different opinion on where the dialogue should've taken place. The characters didn't fit in the picture so we decided to let the stars speak... Literally.
